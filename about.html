About RPCN Abakaliki Parish

Christ is the Head of the Church. We exist to make disciples through faithful preaching, prayer, sacraments, and service.

Our Beliefs

We hold to the authority of Scripture and the historic Reformed confessions. Salvation is by grace alone, through faith alone, in Christ alone.

Our Mission

To glorify God by proclaiming the Gospel, discipling believers, and loving our neighbours in word and deed.
